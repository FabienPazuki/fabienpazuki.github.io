Rational points and Zariski density

Location:

University of Copenhagen: room TBA, Department of Mathematical Science, Universitetsparken 5, 2100 Copenhagen.

Dates:

15-17 November 2017.

Program:

The conference will start at 9:00 on Wednesday 15 and will end at 12:30 on Friday 17. Click HERE for the detailed program.

Organizers:

Sara Checcoli (Univ. Grenoble-Alpes), Fabien Pazuki (Univ. Copenhagen, Univ. Bordeaux).

Registration:

There is no registration fee but if you want to participate we kindly ask you to send an email to fpazuki AT math.ku.dk.

Directions:

The conference will take place in TBA, in the Math Building of Copenhagen University. From the airport, take the metro to Nørreport. From the station, take an S-tog to Nørreport. From Nørreport, grab the bus 150S (or 184, or 185) and stop at Universitetsparken. The Math Building is a small grey cube behind three huge parallele buildings with curious orange small balconies. Nota Bene: on the Saturday morning, main doors will be locked, ask a local.
