On the way to Gothenburg 2019

This conference is a satellite conference of the N-cube Days XI organized in Gothenburg on November 15-16.

Location:

University of Copenhagen: Auditorium 8, HCØ, Nørre Campus, Copenhagen.

Date:

14 November 2019.

Speakers:

  • Francois Brunault (ENS Lyon)
  • Florian Breuer (Newcastle and Stellenbosch)
  • Farbod Shokrieh (Univ Washington)
  • Lars Kuhne (Basel)

Program:

The conference will start at 13:00 on Thursday 14. Here is the detailed program.

Organizer:

Fabien Pazuki (Univ. Copenhagen).

Registration:

There is no registration fee but if you want to participate we kindly ask you to send an email to fpazuki 'at' math.ku.dk.

Directions:

The conference will take place in Auditorium 8, in the HCØ Building of Copenhagen University, close to the Math Department.

Sponsors:

Department of Mathematical Sciences (Denmark).
