Denmark Australia Diophantine Approximation Day

The aim of the meeting is to gather experts studying diophantine approximation and number theory.

Location:

Aarhus University: Auditorium D1, Department of Mathematics, Aarhus University, Ny Munkegade 118, 8000 Aarhus C.

Dates:

8 December 2017.

Speakers:

  • Michael Coons (Univ. Newcastle)
  • Alex Ghitza (Univ. Melbourne)
  • Simon Kristensen (Aarhus Univ.)
  • Fabien Pazuki (Univ. Copenhagen)
  • Evgeniy Zorin (Univ. York)

Program:

The conference will start at 9:00 and will end at 18:00 on Friday 8. Click here for the detailed program.

Organizers:

Simon Kristensen (Aarhus Univ.), Fabien Pazuki (Univ. Copenhagen).

Registration:

There is no registration fee but if you want to participate we kindly ask you to send an email to fpazuki AT math.ku.dk.

Directions:

The conference will take place in Auditorium D1, Department of Mathematics, Aarhus University, Ny Munkegade 118, 8000 Aarhus C. Busses from the train station are 2A, 13 and 16. You should get off at Langelandsgade/Kasserneboulevarden. There's a short walk from the bus stop to the Department.

Sponsors:

Niels Bohr Professorship of Lars Hesselholt (Denmark).
